## Scene 1: Opening

[SCREEN: Navigate to client portal login, log in as a client user]

Your clients want to know their data is safe. Instead of quarterly reports and trust-me emails, give them this — their own compliance portal with real-time visibility.

## Scene 2: Client Dashboard

[SCREEN: Show client dashboard with compliance score, recent activity]

The client sees their compliance health score, their active incidents, and their remediation history. No technical jargon — just clear status. Green means compliant, yellow means in progress, red means attention needed.

## Scene 3: Escalation Control

[SCREEN: Show client escalation preferences]

Clients can choose how they want to be notified. Direct escalation for critical issues. Partner-managed for routine items. Or both. They feel in control without needing to understand the technical details.

[SCREEN: Return to client portal overview]

Transparency builds trust. Trust builds retention. Retention builds revenue. Give your clients a window into their compliance — powered by OsirisCare.
