Let me show you something most MSPs can't do. We're going to watch OsirisCare detect a real compliance drift and fix it automatically — no human intervention, no ticket, no waiting.

## Scene 2: L1 — Deterministic Healing

[SCREEN: Show an L1 incident resolving in the incident list, click into it]

This incident right here — a stopped logging service — was detected by the appliance during its ten-minute scan. Within a hundred milliseconds, our Level One engine matched it against a hundred and twelve deterministic rules and executed the fix. Service restarted. Evidence captured. Done. That handles seventy to eighty percent of all incidents, at zero cost per resolution.

## Scene 3: L2 — LLM Planner

[SCREEN: Show an L2 incident with the AI-generated remediation plan]

Now here's one that was too complex for a simple rule. A misconfigured firewall with multiple dependencies. Our Level Two planner — powered by Claude — analyzed the incident, generated a remediation plan, and scored its confidence at zero point eight. The appliance executed it autonomously. Total cost: about a tenth of a penny. Total time: under five seconds.

## Scene 4: L3 — Human Escalation

[SCREEN: Navigate to escalation queue, show an L3 ticket with full context]

And when it's something that genuinely needs human judgment — a new device type, an unusual configuration — it escalates to Level Three. But look at this ticket. It's not just an alert. It gives you the full incident context, what was tried, why it was escalated, and a recommended action. Your technician spends two minutes instead of twenty.

## Scene 5: The Flywheel

[SCREEN: Navigate to learning/flywheel section]

Here's the magic. Every Level Two resolution feeds our data flywheel. When a pattern succeeds three times with high confidence, it gets promoted to a Level One rule — permanently faster, permanently free. The system literally gets smarter and cheaper over time.

## Scene 6: Close

[SCREEN: Return to dashboard showing resolved incidents]

Three tiers. Seventy percent resolved instantly. Twenty percent by AI in seconds. Ten percent escalated with full context. That's not monitoring — that's autonomous compliance management.
