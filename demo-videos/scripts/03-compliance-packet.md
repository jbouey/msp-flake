## Scene 2: The Packet

[SCREEN: Open a compliance packet, show the summary section]

This is a real compliance packet from last month. It maps fifteen active HIPAA controls to actual evidence from the site. Not checkboxes someone filled in — real scan results, real remediation logs, real timestamps. ## Scene 3: Evidence Bundles

[SCREEN: Scroll through evidence bundles, click into one]

Each evidence bundle is individually signed with Ed25519 cryptography and chained to the previous bundle — like a blockchain for compliance. Over two hundred thousand bundles across our pilot sites. Every single one verifiable, every single one tamper-evident.

## Scene 4: Bitcoin Anchoring

[SCREEN: Show OTS/timestamp proof section]

And for the ultimate in non-repudiation, we anchor evidence to the Bitcoin blockchain using OpenTimestamps. Twenty-seven hundred proofs confirmed so far. This means you can prove to any auditor — years from now — that this evidence existed at this exact time and hasn't been modified.
